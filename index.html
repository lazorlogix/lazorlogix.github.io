About

LAZOR LOGIX is a Rwandan drill artist driven by realism, pressure, and ambition. His music reflects the mindset of a young generation growing up inside strict systems, heavy expectations, and limited opportunities—yet refusing to think small.

Rooted in raw drill energy, LAZOR LOGIX blends local experience with a global sound. His lyrics touch on school life, inner conflict, hustle, betrayal, and silent determination, turning everyday pressure into music that speaks beyond borders.

Rather than chasing trends or validation, LAZOR LOGIX focuses on authenticity. Songs like International and INKOTA highlight a mindset that looks past the present environment toward something bigger.
